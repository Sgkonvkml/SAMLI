SAMLI's story

SAMLI's relentless efforts have been made for high-quality bread. New methods have been developed, such as the “human dough method,” in which molded dough is rapidly frozen and baked directly at the store, and the “wave baking method,” in which the fermented product is baked in half and the rest of the process can be processed at the store. By building trust and developing technology with a differentiated logistics system and the best ingredients, SAMLI, a bakery that you can meet anywhere, was born.
