History

Little bakery that has become our daily life

On our way home, we can easily purchase traditional Parisian baguettes, and choose from a variety of cakes to suit our tastes, considering the ingredients, shapes and decorations. In busy daily life, a simple and healthy meal can be solved with a sandwich made with native yeast bread. This culture, which is now easily accessible, is not a natural thing. It wasn't just food, it started with the mind to fill the heart, to fill the memories, and to fill the small void of happiness. Fresh cream cakes were popular in the cake market, which was mainly butter cream cakes, and various Christmas cakes were popularized.
More than 500 new products are developed a month to showcase differentiated products, but 10% of the products that go out to the store All. Product launches are carried out very carefully because we want to show our customers carefully selected products. So, the opinions of the consumer monitoring panel put into the final section are very valuable.

SAMLI's continued efforts

Even if a million pieces are okay, if one is not okay, it is not safe to bake bread, always recalling the essence of “food” before “products”. These are the top things to remember and don't forget until the end. That's why we are working hard to trust in food that can be eaten with confidence, to obtain ISO 9001 for quality assurance, to strengthen food safety and hygiene, and to run programs for production innovation. With this effort, all bread products at the SAMLI factory have received the HACCP certification, which is a food safety management certification.
