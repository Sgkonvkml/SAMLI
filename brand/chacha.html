CHACHA

CHACHA stands for towering tea, or the best tea, meaning tea of ​​premium quality that was only available in the Chinese imperial family. Taiwan has the best temperature and weather conditions for tea trees to grow. It was able to produce the finest tea leaves for the Chinese imperial family. It is CHACHA, a freshly brewed tea from Taiwan's premium leaf tea, which had been presented to the Chinese imperial family.
